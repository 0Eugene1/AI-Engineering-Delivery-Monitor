# ADR-010: People screen and Notifications out of MVP

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Status** | Accepted |
| **Date** | 2026-07-09 |
| **Related** | [ux.md](../ux.md), [roadmap.md](../roadmap.md), [vision.md](../vision.md) |

## Context

Текущая боль — прозрачность **задач** и релиза, не нагрузка людей и digests.

## Decision

Вне MVP:

- экран People / WIP
- Notification Service (Slack/Teams digests)

Фокус MVP: Sprint Board, Issue Timeline, Activity Feed, Release Health.

## Consequences

Быстрее до ценности на стендапе. People/notifications — после пилота.
